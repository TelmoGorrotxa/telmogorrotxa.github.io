Bio / CV

About Telmo

Telmo Gorrochategui is a classical percussionist and timpanist from the Basque Country, currently based in the Netherlands. He studied classical percussion at the Conservatorium van Amsterdam, where he worked with teachers such as Bence Major, Mark Braafhart, Bart Jansen, Richard Jansen and Rachel Xi Zhang.

Telmo is active as an orchestral player and freelancer, and has performed with ensembles and orchestras including Het Balletorkest, Antwerp Symphony Orchestra, Amsterdam Sinfonietta and members of the Concertgebouw Orchestra. His musical interests range from symphonic repertoire to new music, chamber music and world-rhythm street percussion.

In 2025 he won the percussion/timpani audition for a trial period with the Bilbao Orkestra Sinfonikoa (Bilbao Symphony Orchestra), further strengthening his connection between the Dutch and Spanish musical scenes.

Teaching

Teaching profile & vision

As a teacher, Telmo aims to combine a solid technical foundation with musical imagination and a healthy, sustainable approach to practice. His experience ranges from young beginners to advanced students preparing for conservatoire auditions.

Lessons can focus on orchestral repertoire, solo literature, rhythm & coordination, reading skills, audition preparation or general musicianship. A key element in his teaching is helping each student find an individual sound and a personal, convincing musical narrative.

Telmo believes that learning percussion should be both structured and enjoyable: clear goals, efficient practice strategies and honest feedback, combined with curiosity, creativity and joy in playing.

  • Focus areas: orchestral percussion & timpani, solo marimba/vibraphone, rhythm training, audition preparation.
  • Teaching formats: individual lessons, sectional coaching, rhythm workshops for ensembles and street-percussion groups.
  • Method: combination of sound-oriented technique, slow practice, listening, score study and mental preparation.
  • Location: lessons possible in Amsterdam / Bilbao or online (video call).
